Sa7elOnline

Sa7elOnline is a project created and maintained by Lifetime Development Egypt, it connects people who have properties to rent or sale located at the North Coast of the Egyptian borders including all resorts with those who are looking for these properties to spend some time on the beaches in sunny days.

Properties' owners provide contact information (Phone Number, Mobile Phone Number, Email Address, Fax Number) and buyers should contact them through one of these contact options, it's optional for sellers to provide these information and we respect all the users privacy.

Using Sa7elOnline is totaly FREE of charges, however placing advertisments and marking properties as "Featured Properties" will cost a small fee decided by website's administrators through negotiations.

We try to provide as much information about North Coast's resorts as possible through Sa7elOnline to make it easy for users to reach their destinations without any hassles.
